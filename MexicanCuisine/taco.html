Taco

Homemade Tacos are the perfect busy weeknight dinner. These Easy Ground Beef Tacos are better than take-out and ready in minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. 90% to 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 12 taco shells

Instructions

  1. Add the beef to a large skillet over medium-high heat. Break the meat apart with a wooden spoon.
  2. Add the powdered seasoning. Stir well and cook until the meat is cooked through, about 6-8 minutes
  3. Reduce the heat to medium. Add the tomato sauce and water. Cook for about 7-8 minutes
  4. Warm the tacho shells according to packaged directions
  5. Fill the taco shells with your desired filling
